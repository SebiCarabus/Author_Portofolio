My Books

Minerva

Set in a future scarred by interstellar wars and alien mysteries, this story follows Tom, who awakens from a coma in a high-tech hospital only to face unsettling truths about his past, his memories, and the ethics of scientific progress. As he struggles to rebuild his life on the thriving Earth colony Minerva, Tom navigates love, friendship, and the pull of the stars while confronting questions of identity, reality, and human resilience. Blending epic space battles and alien worlds with psychological depth, the narrative explores survival, discovery, and the search for meaning beyond the known universe.
